The problem with scheduling tools:

You have your calendar in one app, scheduling links in another, and tasks in a third.

FluidCalendar combines all three. Your availability is based on your real calendar + scheduled tasks.

**Tweet 3:**
What I built:

→ Shareable booking pages (/book/yourname/meeting)
→ Real-time availability from Google, Outlook, CalDAV
→ Customizable duration, buffers, notice periods
→ Automatic timezone detection
→ Email confirmations with .ics attachments
→ Guest self-cancel via email
